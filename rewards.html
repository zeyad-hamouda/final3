CASHBACK REWARDS

Cashback rewards are a popular perk offered by many credit card issuers, including Legacy Bank. With Legacy Bank's cashback rewards program, customers can earn cash back on their everyday purchases, such as groceries, gas, and dining. The rewards rate varies depending on the category of spending, with some categories offering higher rewards rates than others. Legacy Bank's cashback rewards can be redeemed as a statement credit, check, or gift card, providing flexibility and convenience for customers.
