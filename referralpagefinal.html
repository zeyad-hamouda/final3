Referral Bonuses


Referral bonuses are a common incentive offered by banks to encourage customers to refer new clients. Existing customers earn rewards for referring friends or family to open an account, while the new customer may also receive a bonus. This win-win arrangement helps banks acquire new customers and rewards existing ones for their loyalty and referrals. Referral bonuses can come in various forms, such as cash rewards or account credits.
